Steps

Sit comfortably and focus on the first bead on the stick of the device held at arm's length.

Slowly bring it closer to your eyes while maintaining focus.

Hold the object at the closest point where it's still clear for 5 seconds.

Slowly move the object away from your eyes while keeping it in focus.

Now move it up and focus for 5 seconds.

Slowly bring it down and focus for 5 seconds.

Repeat this movement 3 times, taking breaks as needed.

Remember to breathe steadily and relax your eye muscles between repetitions.
